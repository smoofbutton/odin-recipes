Beefaroni Recipe

Description

If you like cheap, canned products, you will enjoy this beefaroni.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of beefaroni

Steps

  1. Empty contents of the can into a bowl or microwavable receptacle of your choosing.
  2. Microwave on HIGH for 1 minute 30 seconds or until warm.
  3. Stir thoroughly.
  4. Enjoy!
