Buffalo Chicken Sandwich

Description

This very simple recipe is perfect for beginners who love buffalo chicken. If you want a fast and easy buffalo chicken sandwich, I strongly recommend trying this out!

Ingredients

Four Servings

  • 4 Hamburger Buns, split length-wise
  • 4 skinless, boneless thawed chicken breast halves
  • 1 cup bottled buffalo wing sauce
  • 4-8 teaspoons olive oil / melted butter
  • 4 lettuce leaves (optional)
  • 4 slices of tomato (optional)
  • 4 tablespoons of blue cheese salad dressing

One Serving

  • 1 Hamburger Buns, split length-wise
  • 1 skinless, boneless thawed chicken breast halves
  • ¼ cup bottled buffalo wing sauce
  • 1-2 teaspoons olive oil / melted butter
  • 1 lettuce leaves (optional)
  • 1 slices of tomato (optional)
  • 1 tablespoons of blue cheese salad dressing

Steps

  1. Brush each chicken breast with wing sauce, then season with salt and pepper.
  2. Grill each chicken breast, basting with wing sauce until fully cooked.
  3. Spread 1-2 teaspoons of olive oil or melted butter onto buns. Place bun butter/oil-side down onto a hot pan and toast until slightly golden-brown.
  4. Build each sandwich from bottom bun to top with lettuce, tomato, chicken breast, and blue cheese dressing.
  5. Enjoy!
